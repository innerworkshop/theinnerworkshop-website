# The Inner Workshop — Website Setup Guide
# Follow these steps IN ORDER to get your CMS working

=======================================================
STEP 1 — Upload your files to GitHub
=======================================================

1. Go to github.com and log in (username: innerworkshop)
2. Click the GREEN "New" button (top left)
3. Name your repository: theinnerworkshop-website
4. Make sure it is set to PUBLIC
5. Click "Create repository"
6. On the next screen, click "uploading an existing file"
7. Drag ALL your website files into the upload area
   (everything inside the theinnerworkshop folder)
8. Scroll down, click "Commit changes"
9. Your files are now safely stored on GitHub ✅

=======================================================
STEP 2 — Connect Netlify to GitHub
=======================================================

1. Log into netlify.com
2. Click "Add new site" → "Import an existing project"
3. Choose "GitHub"
4. Authorise Netlify to access GitHub when asked
5. Find and select your "theinnerworkshop-website" repository
6. Leave all settings as default
7. Click "Deploy site"
8. Your site will be live in about 60 seconds ✅

=======================================================
STEP 3 — Enable Netlify Identity (makes CMS login work)
=======================================================

1. In your Netlify dashboard, click on your site
2. Go to "Site configuration" → "Identity"
3. Click "Enable Identity"
4. Scroll down to "Registration" → set to "Invite only"
   (this means only YOU can log into the CMS)
5. Scroll down to "External providers" → no changes needed
6. Scroll down to "Git Gateway" → click "Enable Git Gateway"
   This allows the CMS to save changes to GitHub ✅

=======================================================
STEP 4 — Invite yourself as admin
=======================================================

1. Still in Identity settings
2. Click "Invite users"
3. Enter: hello@theinnerworkshop.co.uk
4. Check your email — you'll get an invite link
5. Click the link and set a password
6. You are now the CMS admin ✅

=======================================================
STEP 5 — Log into your CMS
=======================================================

1. Go to: https://theinnerworkshop.co.uk/admin
   (or your temporary netlify.app URL while DNS is connecting)
2. Click "Login with Netlify Identity"
3. Enter your email and the password you just set
4. Welcome to your website editor! 🎉

=======================================================
WHAT YOU CAN EDIT IN THE CMS
=======================================================

✅ Your name, title, location, email
✅ Your bio on the About page
✅ Your photo (upload directly)
✅ Your qualifications list
✅ All session prices
✅ Testimonials on the homepage
