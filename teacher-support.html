Teacher Support

Practical wellbeing tools and therapeutic support for teachers and school staff — helping educators manage stress and burnout, and equipping them to support pupil emotional wellbeing in the classroom.

Supporting the People Who Support Children

Teaching is one of the most emotionally demanding professions there is. With over 25 years in education as a teacher, dyslexia specialist, and SENDCo, I understand the unique pressures that school staff face — the relentless pace, the emotional weight of responsibility, and the challenge of supporting children's wellbeing while managing your own.

I offer individual therapeutic support for teachers and school staff, as well as group presentations and professional development sessions on emotional wellbeing frameworks for the classroom.

What I Offer Teachers

Individual Wellbeing Sessions

One-to-one EFT Tapping and RTT Hypnotherapy sessions tailored specifically to the pressures of teaching — managing stress, reducing burnout, processing difficult experiences, and building sustainable emotional resilience.

Classroom Wellbeing Tools

Practical EFT-based frameworks you can use directly with your pupils — simple, evidence-based techniques that take minutes to deliver and can make a meaningful difference to emotional regulation and readiness to learn in the classroom.

School Presentations & CPD

Engaging presentations for staff groups on emotional wellbeing, EFT tapping, and supporting pupil mental health. If you have attended a presentation and would like to find out more or sign up to receive resources and updates, please use the link below.
