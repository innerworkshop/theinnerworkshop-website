Bespoke Audio Recordings

Guided Visualisation & Healing Audios

Personalised guided meditation and self-hypnosis recordings crafted specifically for you — for stress relief, sleep, emotional wellbeing, surgical recovery, and deep healing.

Healing from the Inside Out

Recovery from major surgery or serious illness is about so much more than the physical. Your mind plays a powerful role in how quickly and comfortably your body heals — and that is exactly where these recordings come in. Created personally for you, these bespoke audio recordings combine the gentle power of guided meditation and hypnotherapy to support your body's natural healing process.

Whether you are recovering from cancer treatment, cardiac surgery, orthopaedic procedures, or a serious illness, each recording is tailored to meet you exactly where you are.

What Are the Recordings?

Each audio is individually crafted following a consultation, so it reflects your specific surgery, your personal circumstances, and your own healing journey. Using proven hypnotherapy techniques, the recordings guide you into a deeply relaxed state where your mind can begin to work in harmony with your body's recovery. Clients use them daily — in bed, on the sofa, or whenever rest is needed.

  • Reduce pre- and post-operative anxiety and fear
  • Ease discomfort and support pain management
  • Encourage restful, restorative sleep
  • Build confidence and a positive outlook on recovery
  • Support emotional wellbeing during a difficult time

How It Works

You can receive a bespoke healing recording as a standalone purchase or as part of a hypnotherapy session where we work together to create something deeply personal for you. After a brief consultation to understand your needs, your recording is crafted and delivered directly to you, ready to listen to from the comfort of your own home.

Recordings are also available for general stress relief, sleep support, emotional wellbeing, self-hypnosis, and personalised guided meditation — not only for surgical recovery.

Who Is This For?

These recordings are for anyone navigating the physical and emotional challenges of recovery or daily life, including those healing from:

  • Cancer treatment and chemotherapy recovery
  • Heart surgery and cardiac rehabilitation
  • Hip, knee, and spinal surgery
  • General surgery and serious illness
  • Chronic stress and burnout
  • Sleep difficulties and insomnia
  • Emotional overwhelm and anxiety

You do not need any experience with meditation or hypnosis. All you need is a quiet moment and a willingness to rest.

Can Guided Meditation & Self-Hypnosis Help Recovery After Surgery?

Research consistently shows that the mind-body connection plays a significant role in physical recovery. Guided visualisation and self-hypnosis have been shown to reduce anxiety, lower pain perception, support immune function, and improve sleep quality — all of which are critical to healing. When you are deeply relaxed, your body is in its optimal state for repair and restoration.
