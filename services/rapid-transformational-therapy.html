Rapid Transformational Therapy

RTT Hypnotherapy

A pioneering therapeutic approach combining hypnotherapy, regression, subconscious reframing, NLP, and CBT — designed to create deep, lasting change, often more quickly than traditional talking therapies.

What is RTT Hypnotherapy?

Rapid Transformational Therapy (RTT) is a pioneering approach developed by Marisa Peer over 30 years of clinical practice. It combines the most effective principles of hypnotherapy, psychotherapy, NLP, CBT, and regression work into a structured method designed to identify root causes and create lasting change — often in fewer sessions than conventional therapy.

RTT works by accessing the subconscious mind — where our beliefs, emotional patterns, and automatic responses are stored. In a deeply relaxed, focused state, we go beneath the surface to find and understand the root causes of what you are experiencing, rather than simply managing symptoms.

Regression Therapy & Subconscious Reframing

A key component of RTT is regression — gently revisiting earlier experiences that formed the beliefs currently driving your thoughts, feelings, and behaviour. This is not about reliving painful events; it is about understanding them from a new perspective and reframing the meaning they have held. The subconscious mind responds powerfully to this process, allowing old patterns to dissolve and new, more supportive beliefs to take root.

Unlike traditional hypnotherapy which often relies on suggestion alone, RTT goes to the root cause — changing not just how you feel, but how you see yourself, your identity, and your sense of what is possible for you.

Your Personalised Transformation Recording

Every RTT session includes a bespoke transformation recording — a personalised audio created specifically for you following your session. You listen to this recording daily for 21 days, reinforcing the new beliefs and perspectives at a subconscious level. This is a critical part of the RTT process — the brain needs repetition to form new neural pathways, and the recording ensures that the shifts made in session continue to deepen and consolidate over time.

What Makes RTT Different?

Traditional hypnotherapy focuses primarily on symptom management through suggestion. RTT combines regression, reframing, and powerful suggestion work to address the underlying cause. The result is change that feels authentic and lasting — not just a temporary lift, but a genuine shift in how you experience yourself and the world.
