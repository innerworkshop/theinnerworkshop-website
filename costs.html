How many sessions will I need?

This varies from person to person. Those who haven't had the issue for long, or who have good insight into it, may only need a few sessions. On average, clients have between 3 and 6 sessions. Following any suggestions between sessions will help speed up the process.

Your initial adult session includes:

  • ✓ 30 min intake conversation — we dive into your needs and what you want to transform, tailoring the session for maximum impact. Often done in a 30 minute Zoom call prior to the session.
  • ✓ 60–90 min hypnosis session — accessing your subconscious mind, uncovering root causes and creating lasting transformation
  • ✓ Personalised audio recording (~15 mins) — a bespoke recording to listen to daily for 21 days, supporting the reprogramming work done in session
  • ✓ Weekly follow-up for 3 weeks — a check-in by text or short call (max. 15 mins) so you're supported after your session

I often integrate EFT/tapping as an additional tool within RTT sessions where appropriate.

Child/teen sessions (ages 8–18) include:

  • ✓ Introduction to mindfulness and grounding techniques
  • ✓ The importance of positive self-talk
  • ✓ A short, bespoke meditation recording

How many sessions will I need?

Some clients find that one session is sufficient to see meaningful change. Where it isn't, we can schedule a 2nd and/or 3rd session. It is extremely rare that more than 3 sessions are needed.

Reiki — a complementary addition

Reiki is available as a gentle, optional complement to your therapeutic work. It offers a chance to completely switch off, calm your nervous system, release tension, and deeply relax in a peaceful healing space. Many clients find it a wonderful way to support the emotional work done in EFT or RTT sessions — though it is equally available as a standalone treatment.
